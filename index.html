Who We Are

Shri Banke Bihari Charitable Trust.

Shri Banke Bihari Charitable Trust (Regd.) was founded by four followers of Shri. Dinesh Krishan Ji Maharaj (Vrindavan). The four disciples also founded a samiti called "Radhe Radhe Sewa Samiti (Regd.)" . 

The Radhe Radhe Sewa Samiti was founded in the year 2000. Since 2014 onwards this samiti organizes bhagwat every year in the month of december. We will be building an ashram in vrindhavan and also planing to provide free clothes, food and medicines for poors. We will be also providing a general physician in the ashram and who will take care patients without any cost.
